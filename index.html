My Favorite Animals!


Monkeys!

Monkeys are my absolute favorite animal mostly because of their humanlike capabilities such as showing emotional expressions and having the ability to complete humanlike tasks. Monkeys are part of the primates family and are mostly herbivores with the exception of some species eating small insects and eggs.

A few reasons why I like monkeys

  • Monkeys are nimble
  • Monkeys have interesting habitats
  • Monkeys can express their emotions very well
  • Monkeys are very intelligent comparedto other animals
